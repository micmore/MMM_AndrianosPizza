Taste the tradition in every slice

Welcome to our family-owned pizzeria, located in the heart of Chicago since 1983. We take pride in serving delicious, hand-crafted pizzas made with only the freshest ingredients. Our menu features classic and specialty pies, made to order just for you. From our cozy dining room to our friendly staff, we create a warm and inviting atmosphere that keeps customers coming back generation after generation. Whether you're grabbing a slice for lunch or hosting a family dinner, we guarantee a truly satisfying pizza experience. Thank you for choosing us as your go-to pizzeria in the Windy City.

Experience the Wood-Fired Difference

At our pizzeria, we believe in the traditional art of pizza-making and use a wood-fired oven to bake our pies to perfection. The intense heat from the wood creates a crispy crust, while the smoke infuses the pizza with a unique and delicious flavor. Our skilled pizzaiolos carefully hand-stretch each dough and top it with only the freshest ingredients. The result is a beautifully crafted, mouth-watering pizza that can only be achieved through the use of a wood-fired oven. Visit us and taste the difference that baking with wood makes in every bite.
